Pancakes

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1 1/2 TSP Salt
  • 1 TBSP Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 Baking Soda
  • 2 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Vegitable Oil
  • 1 TBSP Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Cup Apple Cider Vinigar
  • 3 Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in large bowl
  2. Mix milk, oil, vanilla, vingegar, and eggs in small bowl
  3. Fold in wet ingredients until batter is mixed and lumpy
